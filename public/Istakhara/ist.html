    ISTIKHAARA (RELIEF FROM PROBLEMS)

    Alhamdullillah! Hazrat Moulana Bashir Farooqi Qadri treats people through letters, emails and in person at Saylani Welfare Trust. Saylani Welfare Trust's staff works under the supervision of Hazrat & sends the answers to people every day. We fulfill its expenditure through financial assistance of wealthy people from all over the world. Our program (ISTAKHARA) is broadcast on ARY channel, QTV where people from 160 Countries call and get Spiritual and Qura'anic solution for their problems on the spot.

    Further, Saylani Welfare Trust arranges two spiritual gatherings in Karachi. Khatme Qadria Sharif helds on every Sunday from Asr to Maghrib Prayers at Jamia Masjid Hazrat Amir Hamza (RadiAllaho Annho), New Golimar Chowrangi, Karachi. Mehfil -e- Qasida Burda Sharif helds on every first and third week of english calender month after Isha Prayers at Jamia Masjid Eidgah, Jama cloth market, Karachi.

LETTERS BY POST :

The first way of istikhaara is through letters & couriers. Thousands of letters are received concerning the problems people all over the world; Maulana Bashir Farooqui has taken charge of sorting out their problems by the effective way of istikhaara. Alhamd-O-lillah, by the grace of Allah the almighty, the organization receives around 2,500 letters every day. The letters are handled by Maulana Bashir Farooqui & their reply with the Qura’anic Ayahs to the respective person. You can send your letters to following adress.

Saylani Welfare International Trust
A-25, Bahadurabad Chowrangi, Karachi, Pakistan.

Maulana Bashir Farooqui is sitting in Saylani Welfare Trust, Bahadurabad, Karachi in the following timimngs.

Daily after ZUHAR to ASAR prayers.
